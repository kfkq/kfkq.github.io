About

I am Muhammad Gaffar, currently pursuing a PhD in theoretical physics with a focus on many-body physics. This blog is a collection of notes, essays, and mini libraries.

Notes are a study notes, that I hope it will be helpful as study reference. Essays will cover my interests subject (physics, philosophy of science, history, secularism, and Islam). The aim of essay is to review a subject and sharing my thoughts. Mini libraries are simply a books recommendation, and collection of references under particular subject, so I (and visitor) can benefit by navigating through it.
