Faith is Not Blind Conformity

Abstract: The modern phenomenon has already created stereotypes of what faith and religion are, often assumed to be blind conformity or merely following traditions that undermine reason. In fact, true faith only exists after certainty arises, and one cannot claim faith is valid without certainty, both in a rational and spiritual sense. Not many religions encourage knowledge, reason, and breaking away from authoritative traditions, but Islam does.

The Importance of Theory of Knowledge

Abstract : I express the importance of theory of knowledge (epistemology) as acquisition and justification of belief systems. This essay emphasizes the significance of epistemology as a foundational tool for understanding the limitations of knowledge, and how to navigate the abundance of information and knowledge in the modern world. Epistemological inquiry is path of humility, yet path to the truth.
