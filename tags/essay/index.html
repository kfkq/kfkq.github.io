The Importance of Theory of Knowledge

Abstract : I express the importance of theory of knowledge (epistemology) as acquisition and justification of belief systems. This essay emphasizes the significance of epistemology as a foundational tool for understanding the limitations of knowledge, and how to navigate the abundance of information and knowledge in the modern world. Epistemological inquiry is path of humility, yet path to the truth.
