It's all about dynamics

February 18, 2024 · 238 words · #physics #shorts

Abstract : Phenomena as we perceive it are dynamics in essence.

only dynamics matter

See the video below. You can observe the octahedron, but if you pause it, can you still see it?

I think video above is simplest way to show that in physical phenomena, or how do we perceive observable in our subjective reality must be understood through the dynamics. The statics doesn’t have any information, but the dynamics does.

In physics the phenomena is encoded in it’s equation of motion, Hamiltonian, or Lagrangian. but often beginner or physics students are blinded that the real physics are in static information, i.e the solution of equation, it’s not. Everything that we measure, even the snapshot of camera, are the result of response function, which trigger dynamics of the system. Everything all over the world is about change, either change of time, space, direction, energy, speed, or else, except in very few cases1.

If we are not getting the full dynamics info, at least, we need to know the correlation through the statistics of configuration distribution. again, either the correlation in time, or correlation in space.

  1. One of the example is the Bose-Einstein Condensate, which the static solution is very special. Another observable that is special without dynamics is speed of light, maybe the it’s role mostly serve as absolute scale in relative of motion, but the fact we perceive light itself somehow tells us about it’s specialty. ↩︎
