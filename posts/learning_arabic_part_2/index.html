Construct and Genitive (الْمُضَافُ وَالْمُضَافُ أِلَيْهِ)

In english you say “Name of the book”. In arabic the structure is the same way with few grammatical rules are involved. The construction is called as “mudaaf-mudaaf ilayhi”. The first noun ins called “mudaaf” (construct) and second, “mudaaf ilayhi” (Genitive).

The following may be noted:

  1. When a noun occurs as a “mudaaf’, it will never be “ma’rifah”, and it can have any “harakah”
  2. “mudaaf ilayhi” can be in “ma’rifa” form or “nakirah.” Both are allowed depending on what precedes.
  3. The second noun i.e., “mudaaf ilayhi” will always have a “kasraa”
